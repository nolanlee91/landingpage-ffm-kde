Why sellers switch

It's almost never one catastrophic failure.

Most sellers don't switch fulfillment providers because of one catastrophic failure.

They switch because of the slow accumulation of small operational problems — the kind that don't show up on a quarterly review but quietly eat the team's week.

How we run it

One scan-disciplined workflow — receive to handoff to resolved.

Every parcel passes through the same flow at the Vancouver floor. Then realtime tracking, normalized across carriers, exceptions surfaced in one queue.

01 · RECEIVE

Inbound scan & verify

Receiving is barcode-driven. Mismatches are flagged before they hit the shelf.

02 · LABEL

Branded thermal labels

One-click bulk printing from the dashboard. Carrier-aware service tier selection.

03 · PACK

Pack & verify

Pick by bin, verify against the order, seal with branded tape. Scan to advance state.

04 · HANDOFF

Carrier handoff & sync

Sorted by carrier and route. Tracking events stream straight back into the dashboard.

Payment operations

Built for how sellers actually settle in Canada.

EFT, e-Transfer, COD, weekly statements — connected to orders only by reference numbers and email memos. KDEXPRESS turns that thread into a workflow.

01 · ROUTING

Dedicated payment routing

Each customer gets their own reference key and a custom payment notification email — so every inflow lands tagged, not anonymous in a shared inbox.

02 · EXTRACTION

Automated email extraction

Bank notifications, carrier remittance and e-Transfer alerts are parsed automatically — amount, sender, reference number and memo, normalized into one payment event stream.

03 · MATCHING

Customer-level reconciliation

Payments matched to the customer that owns the reference. Intentionally not a perfect order-level accounting allocation — that's bookkeeping, not operations.

The dashboard

An operations dashboard, not a fulfillment portal.

Built in-house for our own operation, then opened to sellers. Every screen earns its place on the warehouse floor.

WORKSPACE · DASHBOARD

Your operation, visible in realtime — in your own workspace.

Each seller gets a dedicated workspace. Today's volume, pipeline state, exception counts and recent batches — refreshed live as your team and ours scan through the day.

  • ✓Per-seller workspace — your team and ours, one operational view
  • ✓Pipeline: Pending → Picked → Packed → Labeled → In Transit → Delivered
  • ✓Live activity log synced bidirectionally with Google Sheets
WORKSPACE · ORDER DETAIL

One click to the full audit trail of a parcel.

Recipient, parcel dimensions, payment state, tracking number, carrier, delivery confirmation — plus the full event log of who scanned what, when. Built for support and dispute resolution, not just shipping.

  • ✓One-click open of carrier tracking page
  • ✓Delivery confirmation timestamp recorded automatically
  • ✓Full event log for support & dispute resolution
WORKSPACE · TRACKING TIMELINE

Six checkpoints, one timeline, every parcel.

Label Printed → Picked Up → In Transit → Arrived at Destination → Out for Delivery → Delivered. Written to the order timeline as soon as it happens on the floor or upstream from the carrier.

  • ✓Six-stage normalized timeline per parcel
  • ✓Cross-carrier event ingestion (Canada Post, Purolator, UPS, FedEx)
  • ✓Customer-facing public tracking link generated automatically
